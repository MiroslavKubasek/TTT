# Main idea
Describe one extreme aspect of the topic, then the complete opposite extreme, and finally defend the golden middle ground.

## Example 1: Topic "Your secret rule for a happy life"
* **Extreme 1:** Live for today – spend, travel, never save.
* **Extreme 2:** Live for the future – deny yourself, save for retirement, postpone everything.
* **Middle:** Pick one thing each week for "now" and one for "ten years from now". Balance over fanaticism.

## Example 2: Topic "What I would tell my younger self today"
* **Extreme 1:** "Do whatever you want, rules are for the weak."
* **Extreme 2:** "Stick to the script – school, job, family, retirement."
* **Middle:** "Do it your way, but learn to love the people who love you." Freedom doesn't have to mean selfishness.

## Example 3: Topic "If you were 18 again"
* **Extreme 1:** I'd treat it as a second chance and do everything differently.
* **Extreme 2:** I wouldn't change a second – my mistakes brought me here.
* **Middle:** I'd keep the foundation but start sooner with the things I truly love. Not more, not less.
