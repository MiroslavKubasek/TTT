# Main idea
Situation – Task – Action – Result. Tell a concrete story: situation, task, action taken, outcome.

## Example 1: Topic "A moment you surprised yourself"
* **S:** I was standing in front of 200 people at a conference.
* **T:** I had 10 minutes to explain a complex project.
* **A:** I ditched the slides and opened with a story about my grandmother.
* **R:** The room laughed, questions kept coming – and I learned that people are pulled by stories, not data.

## Example 2: Topic "The power of small steps"
* **S:** Two years ago I couldn't do a single push-up.
* **T:** I wanted to build fitness without punishing myself.
* **A:** I started with one push-up a day and added one every week.
* **R:** Today I can do 50 in a row – and I learned that discipline beats motivation.

## Example 3: Topic "The biggest myth about success"
* **S:** I believed success meant 12-hour grind days.
* **T:** I was trying to hit growth targets in our startup.
* **A:** I stopped doing everything myself and started delegating.
* **R:** The numbers climbed more in a month than in the previous six – success is about focus, not hours.
