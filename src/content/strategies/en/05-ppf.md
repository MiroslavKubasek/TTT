# Main idea
Present the topic across three time frames: what it used to be, what it is today, where it is going.

## Example 1: Topic "The most valuable skill of the 21st century"
* **Past:** In the 20th century the most valuable skill was memorising facts.
* **Present:** Today the key skill is filtering information and thinking critically.
* **Future:** Tomorrow it will be collaborating with AI while staying deeply human.

## Example 2: Topic "What true friendship means"
* **Past:** Friendship once meant writing letters and waiting weeks for a reply.
* **Present:** Today one quick message is enough – but the depth often slips away.
* **Future:** In the future we will rediscover the value of time spent face to face.

## Example 3: Topic "The power of first impressions"
* **Past:** First impressions used to be about a firm handshake and a proper suit.
* **Present:** Today they are shaped by a digital footprint – avatar, bio, tone of voice.
* **Future:** Tomorrow an algorithm will "know" us before we even meet in person.
