# Main idea
Treat the topic as a challenge. Define the problem, explain its root cause, and propose a viable solution.

## Example 1: Topic "Why silence is underrated"
* **Problem:** We live in constant noise – notifications, music, podcasts all day.
* **Cause:** We are afraid of sitting alone with our own thoughts.
* **Solution:** Schedule 15 minutes of daily silence – no phone, no input. See what surfaces.

## Example 2: Topic "What every student should know when leaving school"
* **Problem:** Graduates don't know how to manage money or time.
* **Cause:** Schools teach facts, not life skills.
* **Solution:** A mandatory "Life 101" course – taxes, relationships, basic mental health, simple finance.

## Example 3: Topic "A day you'd live again"
* **Problem:** We often remember days we'd relive – but not why.
* **Cause:** We never stop to analyse what made them special.
* **Solution:** A nightly one-line reflection. After a year you have a map of your own joy.
