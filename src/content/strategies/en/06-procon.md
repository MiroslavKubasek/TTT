# Main idea
Weigh both sides. Start with pros, then cons, and finish by taking a side with reasoning.

## Example 1: Topic "Is it better to be clever or kind?"
* **Pro clever:** Opens doors to careers, problem-solving and independence.
* **Con:** On its own it can feel cold and detached.
* **Conclusion:** I lean towards kindness – we can always build intelligence, but kindness decides whether people remember how we made them feel.

## Example 2: Topic "The art of saying no"
* **Pro:** Protects your time, energy and authenticity.
* **Con:** It can seem selfish or close doors to opportunities.
* **Conclusion:** An honest "no" is ultimately kinder than a reluctant "yes".

## Example 3: Topic "If you could live anywhere in the world"
* **Pro big city:** Cafés, career, cultural abundance.
* **Con:** Noise, crowds, higher stress and prices.
* **Conclusion:** I'd pick a small seaside town – enough energy, but also room to breathe.
