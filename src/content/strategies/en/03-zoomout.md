# Main idea
Start with a very specific, personal detail and gradually expand the topic to a societal, general, or global perspective.

## Example 1: Topic "A book that changed you"
* **Detail:** When I was 14 I found "The Little Prince" in my library.
* **Wider view:** Years later I noticed managers, teachers and therapists all recommend the same book.
* **Global reach:** Maybe we need fewer productivity manuals and more stories that remind us what really matters.

## Example 2: Topic "Your childhood hero"
* **Detail:** My grandfather could fix anything with a single screwdriver.
* **Wider view:** His whole generation took it for granted that you repair things, not replace them.
* **Global reach:** Today, in a climate crisis, we need to return to that simple wisdom.

## Example 3: Topic "Something you'd learn if time didn't matter"
* **Detail:** I'd love to play the violin like my teacher Karel.
* **Wider view:** Music is the one language every culture understands.
* **Global reach:** If we played more and only listened less, the world would probably be a little more empathetic.
