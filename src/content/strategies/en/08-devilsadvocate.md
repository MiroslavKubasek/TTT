# Main idea
Intentionally take an unexpected or controversial opposing stance on the topic and defend it, possibly with humor.

## Example 1: Topic "The power of small steps"
* **Opposition:** Small steps are overrated.
* **Argument:** Sometimes you need a leap. Relationships, careers, moves – you can't do those "1 % better each day".
* **Punchline:** Here's to big, risky decisions that change your life overnight.

## Example 2: Topic "Why doing nothing sometimes matters"
* **Opposition:** Doing nothing is a luxury for those who already have it made.
* **Argument:** Most people actually need to do more – exercise more, read more, learn more.
* **Punchline:** Instead of a slow morning, knock out five things today and then really enjoy doing nothing.

## Example 3: Topic "The best advice you ever received"
* **Opposition:** The best advice is to give no advice at all.
* **Argument:** Advice is usually someone's life pressed into your very different situation.
* **Punchline:** Listen to questions, not answers. Questions force you to think; advice only comforts you.
